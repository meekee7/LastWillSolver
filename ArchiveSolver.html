Puzzle rules
Construct words with the letters. Each puzzle has at least 40 acceptable words. There are 1.728.000 valid positions, 120 of them will solve this puzzle. No valid position leaves the game in an unsolvable state.
Reaching the closest solution will require at most 16 steps on the shortest path (11-12 on average). Reaching any postion will require at most 28 steps on the shortest path (18-19 on average).

Solving can take a couple of seconds (especially on smartphones). Please be patient.

Morphopolis is a game by MCRO, released for various platforms.
