Last Will - Puzzle Solvers

Bunker

Instructions
Place 7 queens on the 8x8 board so that they cannot threaten each other.
In chess queens can move in horizontal, vertical and diagonal lines.
When you are finished press the button upstairs to check your solution.

This is a slighty simplified variation of the well-known eight queens puzzle.
There are many alternatives to the solution presented here.
